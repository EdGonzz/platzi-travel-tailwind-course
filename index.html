FAQs

What services does PlatziTravel offer?

We specialize in providing customized travel packages, including flights, accommodations, guided tours, and unique experiences. We cater to both individual travelers and groups.

How do I book a trip with PlatziTravel?

You can book a trip directly through our website, by contacting our customer service team via phone or email, or by visiting our office.

How can I pay for my trip?

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and online payment platforms like PayPal. We also offer installment plans for certain packages.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies depending on the package booked. Generally, cancellations made 30 days before departure may be eligible for a partial refund, while later cancellations may incur a higher fee or no refund. We recommend purchasing travel insurance for added protection.

Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive travel insurance options that cover trip cancellations, medical emergencies, and more. We highly recommend purchasing travel insurance for your peace of mind.
